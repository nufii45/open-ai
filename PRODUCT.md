# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

HealthBridge serves Filipino medicine buyers who are comparing a familiar branded medicine with a lower-cost generic option. They may be making a time-sensitive purchase and need a clear, calm way to understand a verified price comparison without receiving medical advice.

## Product Purpose

HealthBridge is a medicine-price transparency prototype. It helps a person search a familiar medicine brand and immediately see a curated, like-for-like generic comparison with the potential PHP savings. Success is a user quickly understanding a verified comparison basis and the savings figure, then knowing to consult a pharmacist or prescriber before changing medicines.

## Positioning

Verified like-for-like generic price comparisons that make potential out-of-pocket savings clear without pretending to give medical advice.

## Brand Personality

Calm, reassuring, and practical. The interface should create financial relief through clarity, never pressure: it is a credible consumer health tool rather than an overly clinical hospital portal or a hard-sell discount app.

## Anti-references

Avoid the dense, jargon-heavy visual language of an overly clinical hospital portal. Avoid fear-based medical copy, urgency-driven savings tactics, dashboard clutter, and playful wellness aesthetics that undermine price and safety information.

## Design Principles

1. Make the verified savings figure immediately legible while keeping the exact comparison basis visible.
2. Use calm, plain language that supports a decision to ask a pharmacist or prescriber rather than suggesting a self-directed switch.
3. Design for one focused mobile task: search, understand the comparison, and leave with the next safe step.
4. Treat provenance, price freshness, and verified status as quiet but easily available trust signals.
5. Make every state—especially no-match and invalid data—feel helpful without inventing medical or pricing claims.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA as a baseline. Support reduced motion, high-contrast readable text, keyboard interaction, and reliable use on budget Android phones and 360px-wide mobile screens.
